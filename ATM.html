Amazing Teachers:

Bancroft's ATM teachers, Kari Milton and Linda McClure are both innovative and inspiring. They create an exciting and safe learning environment for young minds to explore the use of the web. Both teachers have personally helped me excel in the development of useful skills, such as typing, SketchUp, coding, photography, Photoshop, google apps, Illustrator, and Powerpoint. My three years at Bancroft in the ATM program have been exceptional because I feel prepared to enter highschool and complete assignments and projects on the computer. I am so grateful to Ms. Milton and Mrs. McClure for constantly going out of their way to make their lessons engaging and designed to enhance our skill set.
