Something I feel very strongly about is DownSyndrome Awareness. I feel that everyone should be able to dance and have fun no matter what physical or mental challenge someone may face. I have had the oppurtunity to work with a Non-Profit Orginization called "Down 4 Dance" which is a dance program for peole with DownSyndrome. The director Sari Anna Thomas has based this program in Long Beach and Orange Country. I have been able to learn so much from this experience and been able to perfrom at Angel stadium with these amazing people.
